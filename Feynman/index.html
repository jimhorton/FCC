Richard Feynman

Richard Feynman at the Robert Treat Paine Estate in Waltham, Massachusetts, in 1984.

Timeline of Richard Feynman's life:

1918 - Born May 11, 1918 in Queens, New York.

1939 - Feynman earns Bachelor's degree from Massachusetts Institute of Technology.

1942 - Receives Ph.D. from Princeton.

1942 - Richard Feynman marries Arline Greenbaum on June 29, 1942.

1943 - Feynman joins Robert Oppenheimer's team at the Los Alamos Laboratory while working on The Manhattan Project.

1959 - Feynman moves to Caltech to teach.

1988 - After suffering a ruptured duodenal ulcer that caused kidney failure, Feynman dies on February 15, 1988.
